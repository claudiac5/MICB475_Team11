# P08 - Functional Analysis and Profiling of Microbial Communities Within Subtypes


## Purpose:
To evaluate the predicted functional potential of the gastric microbiome between intestinal and diffuse subtypes and determine the extent to which biological sex modifies these functional pathways.

## Material: 
* 16S rRNA gene sequencing dataset (V4 region) from Wang et al. (2020).
  * Filtered feature table (45 samples: 30 diffuse, 15 intestinal).
* The MetaCyc metabolic pathway database.
* The following R packages: phyloseq, tidyverse, ANCOMBC (v2.6.0), ggplot2, and ggrepel.
