# P02 - QIIME 2 Denoising and Clustering 

February 20, 2026

## Purpose:
To remove sequencing errors, trim low-quality bases, and generate amplicon sequence variants (ASVs) using the DADA2 algorithm.

## Material: 
- Demultiplexed sequences
- Sample metadata
- QIIME 2 with DADA2 plugin

## Method:
1. Selected trimming parameters based on the demultiplexing quality plot.
2. Ran DADA2 to denoise sequences and identify ASVs.
3. Generated feature tables and representative sequences.

## Code: 
[Script](../QIIME2%20/Script/Code.R)

## Output Files:
- [rep_seqs.qzv](../QIIME2%20/Export/rep_seqs.qzv)
- [table.qzv](../QIIME2%20/Export/table_gc_intestinal_diffuse.qzv)
   
## Results: 
No trimming was deemed necessary 

## Discussion:
No trimming was performed because the quality scores across reads remained sufficiently high. Retaining the full read length allowed preservation of maximum sequence information, which can improve taxonomic resolution during classification.

## Future direction:
Perform taxonomic classification to assign microbial identities to the ASVs.
